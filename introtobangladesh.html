About Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a beautiful country in South Asia, known for its rivers, green nature, rich culture, and friendly people. It became independent in 1971 and its capital city is Dhaka. Bangladesh has one of the fastest-growing economies in the world and is famous for its garments industry, delicious food, and warm hospitality.

Culture & Traditions

Bangladesh has a rich cultural history with festivals like Pohela Boishakh, Ekushey February, and Victory Day. Traditional foods include biriyani, pitha, hilsa fish, and sweets. The country is also known for its music, literature, and colorful clothing.
